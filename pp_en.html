    2. Handling of Image Content in Standard App Features
    In this application, all image content input by users is processed only within the device. 
    These contents are never sent to our server under any circumstances, and external transfer or provision to third parties will not occur.

    3. Handling of Image Content in Watermark Distribution Features
    In this service, image content uploaded by managing users is stored in a database storage contracted by us. 
    It is also synchronized with the deletion operations of managing users, and will be deleted from the database storage upon deletion.
    Regarding these contents, they will not be transferred externally or provided to third parties by our operations, except for downloading and using by general users in accordance with the terms.

    4. Acquisition of User Information via Information Collection Modules
    This application does not use information collection modules.

    5. Provision of User Information to Third Parties
    Since this application does not send information to the developers' server, we do not disclose or provide information to third parties.

    6. Publication of Privacy Policy
    The privacy policy will be published in the "Privacy Policy Screen" of the "User Edit Screen" of this application and at the URL (https://).
